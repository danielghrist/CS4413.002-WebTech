Hobbies & Interestsi'm a hermit

My hobbies are currently pretty limited as I don't have much free time with school and life issues that pop up. I enjoy collecting retro video games, mostly with a focus on Nintendo handhelds. I have a near complete NTSC collection for the DS and 3DS. Plus some other consoles. I have started getting rid of games for consoles I don't care about, like the Wii. Overall I have over 2,000 games or so. I have had to put the collecting on hold though as prices are crazy and I don't have an income. I enjoy watching weird documentaries and edutainment content on YouTube as well. However, I am mostly just doing either school, or random chores, or dealing with issues that pop up on a day to day basis. Oh I guess eating is a hobby. Oh how I love eating.
